Oakland University Geofilters

  • Client: Oakland University
  • Category: Layouts
  • Project URL: oakland.edu/social

Snapchat Geofilters created for OaklandU Snapchat Geofilter Contest in 2016. The guidelines were as follows:

• Create an original Snapchat geofilter according to Snapchat’s guidelines for a 'Community' geofilter
• The geofilter must be for intended use on Oakland University’s campus
• Each participant may submit up to two geofilters for the contest: 1 general OU campus geofilter and 1 geofilter for a specific location on campus (such as an academic building or point of interest)

The geofilter created for Kresge Library was chosen for Honorable Mention in the contest.
