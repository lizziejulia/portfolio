Devin Attard is a drummer based out of Detroit, MI and is currently creating content and working as a session drummer. He's currently endorsed by Vic Firth, Paiste Cymbals, SJC Custom Drums, Gibraltar Hardware, and VRATIM.
Previously, he has been a drummer for multiple bands including Here We Harbour, Hollow Front, Illustrator, Of Virtue, Boys of Fall, and Hotel Books. Devin was looking for a simple and minimalistic logo including his initials.
